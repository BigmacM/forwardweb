The honest version

One person, building your whole site, on purpose

ForwardWeb is a brand-new, founder-led studio — so I'll be straight with you about what that means.

When you hire an agency, you usually meet a senior closer and then get handed to whoever's free. When you buy a cheap template, you get a site that looks like ten thousand others and slows to a crawl on a phone. ForwardWeb is the middle path: a custom site, hand-built by one person who answers their own phone, at a fixed price you agree to up front.

I'm based in Vaughan and I build for Vaughan and the wider GTA. Because there's no hand-off, nothing gets lost in translation — your goals, your brand and your deadline stay front and centre from the first call to launch day. And because I'm just getting going, every project genuinely matters. I'd rather earn your trust with the work than borrow it with fake reviews.
