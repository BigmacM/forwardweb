Our story

Built on a simple idea: local businesses deserve better websites

Too many Vaughan businesses get stuck with slow, dated websites that don't show up on Google and don't bring in customers — often after paying an agency a small fortune for it.

ForwardWeb started to fix that. We're a small, founder-led studio focused on one thing: building fast, modern websites that actually grow local businesses. Because you deal directly with the person building your site, nothing gets lost in translation — your goals, your brand and your deadlines stay front and centre from the first call to launch day.

We're proudly based in Vaughan and we build for Vaughan. We know the neighbourhoods, the local search landscape and what makes capital-region customers click "call" instead of scrolling past. That local context is baked into every site we ship.
