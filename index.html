FAQ

Questions Vaughan businesses ask us

How much does a small business website cost in Vaughan?

Most ForwardWeb websites range from $1,500 to $5,000 depending on the number of pages, features, and whether you need e-commerce. You get a fixed quote up front — no surprises, ever.

How long does it take to build a website?

A typical small business site launches in 2 to 4 weeks from kickoff. Larger or e-commerce projects take a little longer. We agree on a clear timeline before we start.

Will my website actually show up on Google?

Yes. Every site is built SEO-first — fast load times, clean structure, local schema markup, and proper on-page optimization so Vaughan customers can find you when they search.

Do I have to handle the technical stuff?

No. We handle the domain, hosting, build, launch and Google setup. You focus on running your business — we'll even train you on simple edits if you want them.

What if I already have a website?

Great — we'll audit it and either improve it or rebuild it into something faster and better-ranking, while protecting the search rankings you already have.
