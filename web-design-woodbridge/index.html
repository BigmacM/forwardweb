Built for Woodbridge

A site with the same warmth as the business behind it

Woodbridge has a feel you don't find everywhere. It's the heart of Vaughan's Italian-Canadian community, and so much of the business here is family-run — restaurants, bakeries, contractors and trades passed down and built on reputation, word of mouth and decades of doing right by people. Around Market Lane and Islington Ave, the shops have stories. Your website should sound like that, not like a stock template.

The trouble is, a lot of those great local businesses have a website that doesn't do them justice — slow, dated, hard to read on a phone, and nowhere to be found when someone searches. Meanwhile the next generation of customers checks online first. That gap is exactly what I close.

Because I'm based next door in Vaughan, working with Woodbridge owners is easy and personal. I write copy that carries the trust you've already earned in the neighbourhood, and I set things up so people searching nearby actually land on you.
